When you're interviewing for a job, you know you need to sell the hiring manager on why your background, kills and industry experience are a match for the position. But there's one crucial piece of the puzzle that you shouldn't overlook: Is your personality right for the job? No two people are identical; certain personality traits can make people similar or dissimilar to others. The importance of different personalitie in the workplace can be traced to several different factors. A team made up of several different compatible personalities that complement one another can be more productive than a team made up of those who are so similar they cannot work productively together.
